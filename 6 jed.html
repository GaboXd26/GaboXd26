About Me!

My name is Jedric Gitalla, and I’m 18 years old. I’m a student studying Information and Communications Technology (ICT). I love learning about how technology works and how it affects our lives. I’m still getting better at my skills, but I enjoy exploring different parts of ICT, like programming, fixing problems, and networking. I believe technology is important in our daily lives, and I want to keep learning so I can use what I know in the future.

Besides studying, I like playing video games and trying new things. I enjoy discovering new technologies and learning new skills. I like spending time alone because it helps me focus and be creative. However, being shy makes it hard for me to talk to people and socialize.

Sometimes, my shyness makes it hard for me to express myself, which can affect my schoolwork. I find it tough to speak up in class or ask for help, but I’m trying to get better at this by pushing myself a little at a time. I know that building my confidence will help me in school and in my future job. Even with these challenges, I am determined to grow and improve myself every day.
